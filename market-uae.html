    Market Context

    UAE — A Re-Export Gateway

    UAE buyers are not just end-consumers. Dubai and Jebel Ali function as the world's largest re-export hub. A single UAE trading relationship can supply Kenya, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Saudi Arabia simultaneously through one buyer. This is why UAE is a strategic priority market — not just a destination, but a distribution network.

    Working With UAE Buyers

    What UAE Buyers Expect

    Speed of Response

    UAE traders move fast. Pricing windows are short. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours with product specs, availability, and preliminary pricing — so you can make decisions without waiting.

    CIF Pricing Preferred

    Most UAE buyers prefer CIF Jebel Ali — they want a single landed cost number. We provide both FOB Mundra and CIF Jebel Ali quotes on request so you can compare total cost of import.

    Clean Documentation

    UAE Customs and re-export documentation requirements are strict. We provide a complete document set: BL, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, COO, Phytosanitary Certificate, and COA on all shipments.

    Multi-Product Orders

    UAE trading houses frequently consolidate multiple commodities. We can coordinate multi-product FCL shipments — rapeseed meal and sesame in the same container, for example — with clean separate documentation per product.
