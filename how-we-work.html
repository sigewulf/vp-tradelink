    How We Work

    A straightforward 6-step process — from your first inquiry to cargo delivery at your port.

    Our Process

    From Inquiry to Delivery

    01

    Send Your Requirement

    Share your product, specification, quantity, and destination port. Email or inquiry form — we respond within 1 business day (Mon–Fri, IST).

    02

    Receive Our Offer

    We send a formal offer: product spec sheet, FOB/CIF price, MOQ, lead time, payment terms, and available inspection options.

    03

    Confirm & Contract

    On agreement, we issue a Sales Contract or Proforma Invoice. Payment terms confirmed. Production or sourcing begins.

    04

    Production & Inspection

    Product is sourced, processed, and packed to your specification. Third-party inspection (Geochem/SGS) arranged if required — at buyer's cost.

    05

    Shipment & Documentation

    Cargo loaded at Mundra or Kandla. Full document set dispatched: BL, Invoice, Packing List, COO, Phytosanitary Certificate, COA.

    06

    Delivery & Support

    Vessel tracking shared. Documents couriered or sent via bank (LC). Post-shipment support for any documentation queries.

    Lead Time 20–45 Days Subject to product availability and port schedules
    Payment Terms LC · TT · Advance Negotiable, based on buyer profile and order history
    Quality Assurance Third-Party Inspection Geochem & SGS accepted — buyer arranges & bears cost
    What We Handle

    Everything Between Factory and Your Port

    You place the order. We handle the rest — sourcing, quality, packing, documentation, and shipping.

    Sourcing & Quality

    We source from established, verified Indian manufacturers. Product is cross-checked against your specification before loading. No substitutions without prior approval.

    Export Documentation

    Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, COA — all prepared accurately and dispatched on time.

    Logistics & Shipping

    We coordinate booking, stuffing, port procedures, and customs at Mundra (INMUN) and Kandla (INIXY). Vessel tracking shared once BL is issued.

    Transparent Pricing

    Price quoted at enquiry stage — not revised after negotiation. FOB Mundra and CIF destination quoted together so you can make a clear landed cost comparison.

    Inspection Coordination

    Geochem and SGS inspection accepted on all products. Buyer arranges inspection and bears cost. We provide inspector access at loading point without delay to schedule.

    Relationship First

    We are not a marketplace — we are a sourcing partner. Once we work together, you have a direct line to India's manufacturing and agricultural supply chain.

    Incoterms & Payment

    Commercial Terms We Work On

    Incoterms

    We offer FOB and CIF only

    FOB Mundra / Kandla — Risk and cost transfer at origin port rail. You arrange freight and insurance. Best if you have a freight contract in place.

    CIF [Destination Port] — We arrange freight and insurance to your port. One total landed cost. Best for first-time buyers or smaller volumes.

    Payment Terms

    LC · TT Advance · CAD

    LC at Sight — Preferred for new buyers. Protects both parties. We accept LC from all major banks.

    TT Advance — 30–50% advance with balance before BL release. For established relationships.

    CAD — Documents against payment. Available for selected buyers on agreed terms.
