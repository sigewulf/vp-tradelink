    Who We Serve

    Industries

    Animal Nutrition & Feed

    Feed mills run on tight production schedules. A late shipment or off-spec protein percentage shuts your line down. We supply rapeseed meal, soybean meal, and DORB to agreed specification with documentation ready before vessel departure — not chased after.

    Ceramics & Glass

    A high Fe₂O₃ reading in quartz ruins a production batch. We supply from beneficiated Rajasthan sources with COA on every shipment — so your quality control team has data before the container arrives, not after it opens.

    Spice & Food Processing

    Purity rejection at destination is expensive — product returned, reputation affected. Cumin and sesame we supply are machine cleaned and sortexed to buyer-specified purity, with phytosanitary certificate issued pre-shipment.

    Pharma & Nutraceutical

    Regulatory-grade psyllium husk buyers need documented swelling factor and ash content — not supplier estimates. We provide Geochem-tested COA with every batch so your formulation team and regulatory affairs have what they need on day one.

    Paints & Coatings

    Inconsistent mesh distribution in quartz powder affects paint viscosity and finish quality. We supply to specified mesh range with batch COA — so your formulation stays consistent from the first FCL to the fiftieth.

    Trading & Distribution

    Re-exporters and trading houses need a supplier who does not compete with their end-buyers. We supply FOB only to traders who handle their own destination relationships — clean, no conflict, no communication to your end-customers.
