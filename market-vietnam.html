    Buyer Behavior

    Working With Vietnamese Buyers

    Specification First

    Vietnamese feed mill procurement managers compare specifications before prices. Send your spec sheet — protein percentage, moisture, fiber — before any price discussion. A buyer who receives a detailed COA-aligned spec sheet from you will respond. A generic price without specs gets ignored.

    Relationship-Based Buying

    Once a Vietnamese buyer trusts a supplier, they rarely switch. First orders are small — often 1–2 FCL as a trial. Deliver exactly to specification on the trial shipment and you build a long-term, repeat-order relationship. Miss on the trial and you will not get a second chance.

    Email Is Preferred

    Serious procurement managers at major Vietnamese feed mills (CJ Vina, Greenfeed, Japfa Comfeed Vietnam, Haneco) use official company email for first contact and formal correspondence. Professional, specific, brief email outreach works. Keep subject lines commercial: product name, origin, specification, your port.

    Response Within 1 Business Day

    Vietnamese buyers are accustomed to fast responses from Chinese and Thai suppliers. We respond within 1 business day with availability, indicative specification, and preliminary pricing — so Vietnamese buyers receive the same speed from India that they expect from closer origins.

    Importing to Vietnam?

    Share your product, quantity, and port of discharge. We respond within 1 business day with specifications and pricing.
