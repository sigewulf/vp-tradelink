Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to respond to an inquiry?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Initial response includes product availability, specifications, and preliminary pricing.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Include product type, required quantity, quality specifications, packaging preference, delivery timeline, and your location. The more detailed your inquiry, the faster we can provide accurate quotations.

Do you provide samples?

Sample availability depends on the product. Contact us with your requirements and we'll advise on sample feasibility and logistics.

What payment terms do you offer?

We work with standard international payment methods: Advance Payment, Letter of Credit (LC), and Documentary Credit. Terms are negotiated based on buyer and supplier requirements.

Can you arrange shipping?

Yes, we coordinate all logistics including containerised shipments, freight forwarding, customs clearance, and delivery. We work with FOB and CIF Incoterms.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Standard MOQ is one 20-foot container (20 FT) or as mutually agreed based on product and specification. Contact us for specific details.
