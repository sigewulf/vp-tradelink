    Beyond Our Primary Markets

    We Ship Worldwide

    Our six primary market pages cover our highest-volume destinations. We also regularly ship to — or can accommodate buyers in — these markets.

    Middle East Extended

    Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman — all served via CIF or through UAE re-exporters. Feed ingredients and agro commodities with Halal documentation available.

    East Africa

    Kenya, Tanzania, Ethiopia — growing feed and agro import markets. Longer transit via Mombasa or Dar es Salaam. Pre-shipment inspection often required.

    South Asia

    Sri Lanka, Nepal — smaller volumes, shorter transit. Ideal for LCL consolidations or as secondary orders alongside larger FCL shipments to larger markets.

    Europe

    Germany, Netherlands, UK — primarily for premium agro commodities. Sesame, cumin, and psyllium have established European import markets. EU food safety and MRL compliance required.

    Southeast Asia Extended

    Thailand, Malaysia, Myanmar — feed ingredients and agro commodities. Growing compound feed sectors with demand for Indian protein meal and spices.
