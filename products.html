Rapeseed Meal

Product Overview

Rapeseed meal is a high-protein byproduct from rapeseed oil extraction. As a rich source of crude protein and amino acids, it is widely used as a primary ingredient in compound animal feeds for poultry, swine, cattle, and aquaculture. Available from established Indian oil processing mills.

Key Applications

  • Poultry feed formulations (layers, broilers)

Product Frequently Asked Questions

What quality standards do your products meet?

All products are sourced from established manufacturers and meet international export standards. Quality is verified through testing and third-party inspection upon request. Complete analysis reports and certificates of origin are provided with every shipment.

Can I customize specifications?

Yes, specifications can be customized according to your requirements. Contact us with your specific needs and we will work with our supplier network to meet your exact specifications for protein content, particle size, moisture, or other parameters.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Standard minimum order is one 20-foot container (20 FT) which holds approximately 20-24 MT depending on product density. For specific products or smaller quantities, contact us to discuss possibilities.

How is product quality ensured?

We work with verified suppliers, conduct pre-shipment quality checks, and arrange independent third-party inspection upon request. All products are tested for specifications, purity, and compliance before loading into containers.

Do you provide samples?

Sample availability depends on the product and quantities. Contact us with your requirements and we will advise on sample feasibility, handling, and shipping arrangements.

What payment terms do you offer?

We work with standard international payment terms: Advance Payment, Letter of Credit (LC), and Documentary Credit (DC). Payment terms are negotiated based on buyer profile and order size.

How long does sourcing and shipment take?

Typical timeline is 20-45 days from inquiry to shipment, depending on product, quantity, and specifications. This includes supplier selection, production, quality assurance, documentation, and port loading.

Can you handle international shipping?

Yes, we coordinate complete export logistics. We arrange containerized freight, manage shipping insurance, handle customs documentation, and work with freight forwarders and port operators globally.

What Incoterms do you use?

We work with FOB (Free on Board) and CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) Incoterms. Specific Incoterms are negotiated and finalized during commercial confirmation.

Do you provide certificates and documentation?

Yes, all shipments include certificates of origin, phytosanitary certificates (where applicable), inspection reports, commercial invoices, packing lists, and bill of lading. Additional certifications available upon request.
