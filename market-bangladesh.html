    Sourcing for Bangladesh

    We supply Rapeseed Meal, Soybean Meal, Sesame Seeds, and Agro Commodities from India to feed mills, trading houses, and importers across Bangladesh — primarily via Chittagong port.

    Market Context

    Bangladesh — India's Closest High-Volume Feed Market

    Bangladesh is one of South Asia's fastest-growing animal feed markets. Poultry, dairy, and aquaculture industries are expanding rapidly. The country imports large volumes of rapeseed meal and soybean meal annually — and India is the natural supply origin due to geographic proximity, competitive freight, and established trade routes.

    Working With Bangladesh Buyers

    What Bangladesh Buyers Expect

    Price-Competitive but Quality-Conscious

    Bangladesh feed mill buyers are price-sensitive but not reckless. They will not sacrifice protein percentage for a slightly lower price — a bad batch shuts a production line down. They want your spec sheet before they discuss price.

    LC is Standard

    Letter of Credit (LC at sight) is the standard payment method for Bangladesh. Most importers work through established banks — Islami Bank, Dutch-Bangla, BRAC Bank. We are LC-ready and comfortable with LC documentation.

    Consistent Supply Matters More Than Lowest Price

    Bangladesh feed mills run on production schedules. An exporter who disappears after the first shipment costs them more than paying slightly higher to a reliable supplier. Demonstrate reliability early — response speed, document accuracy, and delivery timing.

    Geochem & SGS Accepted

    Third-party inspection at origin (Geochem or SGS) is accepted on all shipments. Buyer arranges and bears inspection cost. We facilitate access for inspectors at the loading point — no delays to shipment schedule.
