    About VP TRADELINK

    Gujarat-based commodity sourcing and export company. Established 2019. Shipping via Mundra and Kandla to buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.

    Who We Are

    Established 2019. Gujarat, India.

    VP TRADELINK is a Gujarat-based international commodity sourcing and export company established in 2019. We specialize in Feed Ingredients, Industrial Minerals, and Agro Commodities — sourcing from verified Indian manufacturers and processors and supplying to international buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and Europe.

    We operate through Mundra (INMUN) and Kandla (INIXY) ports. Our inquiry-to-response time is within 1 business day (Monday to Friday, IST). Third-party inspection by Geochem and SGS is accepted on all shipments — arranged and borne by the buyer. We supply exclusively on FOB and CIF terms, with payment via LC, TT advance, or CAD based on buyer profile.

    We are an IEC-registered exporter under DGFT, Gujarat. Our approach is direct, transparent, and focused on building long-term supply relationships — not one-time transactions.

    Inside Our Sourcing & Dispatch Process

    Our Position

    Where We Fit in the Supply Chain

    Sourcing Intermediary

    We are not a manufacturer. We identify, qualify, and coordinate supply from established Indian producers — giving buyers a single point of contact for multiple commodities across multiple source categories.

    Export Coordinator

    We manage the entire export process — from packing and inspection to documentation, customs clearance, and vessel booking. The buyer deals with us; we handle the India-side complexity.

    Price Transparency

    Our margin is built into the FOB price we quote — not hidden in add-on charges. You receive one all-in price. No surprises at shipment stage.

    Scope Clarity

    What We Do Not Do

    Being clear about our limits protects your time and ours. Read this before you inquire.

    We do not manufacture

    We are a sourcing and export intermediary. We source from established Indian producers and processors. Pricing reflects market rates — not factory-gate margins.

    We do not handle retail quantities

    Our minimum order is one full container load (FCL). We are not set up for sample-only orders, small parcels, or retail quantities. Serious B2B importers only.

    We do not hold prices open-endedly

    Commodity prices move daily with market conditions. A price quoted today is valid for the period stated in the formal offer — typically 48 to 72 hours. We cannot hold pricing beyond the stated validity.

    We do not arrange buyer financing

    We accept LC, TT advance, and CAD — but do not extend credit facilities or deferred payment to buyers. For import financing, consult your bank or trade finance institution.
