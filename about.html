About VP TRADELINK

We coordinate global sourcing, quality assurance, export documentation, and logistics—enabling international buyers to source confidently from India.

Who We Are

VP TRADELINK is an India-based global sourcing partner specializing in Feed Ingredients, Industrial Minerals, and Agro Commodities. We coordinate sourcing, quality assurance, export documentation, and logistics—connecting international buyers with trusted Indian manufacturers and processors.

Our commitment is to professionalism, transparency, and dependable execution. We focus on building long-term business relationships through consistent quality, reliable service, and clear communication.

What We Do

Global Sourcing

Reliable sourcing through trusted Indian manufacturers and processors according to your specifications.

Quality Assurance

Products supplied according to mutually agreed specifications. Independent third-party inspection can be arranged upon request.

Export Coordination

Professional management of documentation, shipment planning, export compliance, and logistics.

Long-Term Partnerships

Focused on transparency, consistency, and building lasting business relationships.

Our Sourcing Network

VP TRADELINK works with a trusted network of manufacturers and processors across India's major sourcing centers for Feed Ingredients, Industrial Minerals, and Agro Commodities. This enables us to source products according to specifications while maintaining consistent quality and reliable supply.

Global Markets

We serve importers, manufacturers, distributors, and industrial buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and other international markets. Our experience in international trade enables us to manage sourcing and shipments to customers worldwide.
