    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: June 2026 (draft)

    This Privacy Policy explains what personal data VP TRADELINK ("we", "us") collects through vptradelink.com, why we collect it, and what choices you have. It applies to visitors and to anyone who submits an inquiry through our contact form.

    1. Who we are

    VP TRADELINK is a sourcing and export company headquartered in Gujarat, India. For any privacy question, correction request, or complaint, contact us at shezad@vptradelink.com. We aim to respond to privacy requests within a reasonable time, generally within 30 days.

    2. What we collect

    When you submit our contact form, we collect: your full name, company name, country, email address, product interest, and any details you write in your message. If you arrive at the contact form by clicking a product card, the specific product you clicked is also recorded.

    3. Why we collect it

    We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry, prepare quotations, and follow up on potential business with you. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use it for unrelated marketing without separate consent.

    4. Legal basis

    We process your data based on the consent you give when you tick the consent box and submit the form, and, where applicable, our legitimate interest in responding to a business inquiry you initiated.

    5. Third parties who process your data

    Form submissions are delivered through FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party email delivery service, which may process and store data outside India, including in the United States. Our website also loads fonts from Google Fonts (Google LLC), which may set technical cookies as part of delivering the font files.

    6. International transfer

    Because FormSubmit and Google Fonts are not India-based, some data may be transferred outside India in order to operate this website and respond to your inquiry. We take reasonable steps to use established, reputable providers, but we have not yet executed formal data processing agreements with these providers. [To be completed — review whether a Data Processing Agreement with FormSubmit is required under GDPR for EU visitors.]

    7. How long we keep it

    Inquiry data is retained for up to 12 months from the date of submission, after which it is deleted unless an active business relationship requires it to be kept longer.

    8. Your rights

    You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or withdraw consent at any time, by emailing shezad@vptradelink.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

    9. Cookies

    This site does not set first-party tracking or advertising cookies. Google Fonts may set a limited technical cookie when font files are delivered. See our Cookie Policy for details.

    10. Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
